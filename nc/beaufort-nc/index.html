Beaufort, NC Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Beaufort (/ˈboʊfərt/ BOH-fərt) is a town in and the county seat of Carteret County, North Carolina, United States. Established in 1713 and incorporated in 1723, Beaufort is the fourth oldest town in North Carolina (after Bath, New Bern and Edenton). On February 1, 2012, Beaufort was ranked as 'America's Coolest Small Town' by readers of Budget Travel Magazine.
