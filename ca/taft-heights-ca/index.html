Taft Heights, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Taft Heights (formerly, Boust City) is a census-designated place (CDP) in Kern County, California, United States. Taft Heights is located 1 mile (1.6 km) west-southwest of Taft, at an elevation of 1,178 feet (359 m). The population was 1,949 at the 2010 census, up from 1,865 at the 2000 census.
